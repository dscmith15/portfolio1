Results of Phase 3 (2 Week Delay and $20)

We collected 50 participants using Amazon's Mechanical Turk to investigate delays and values that are more consistent with experiences in free-to-play games. Specifically, the maximum delay was changed to 2 weeks compared to the previous phases' maximum delay of 5 years. In addition, a prospect of $20 replaced the $40k used in the two previous phases.

Key Takeaway: Participants made significantly riskier decisions with the $20 prospect
