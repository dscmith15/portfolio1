Pilot for Replication of Vanderveldt, A., Green, L., & Myerson, J. (2015).

This is the first phase of my dissertation. We are replicating a study by Vanderveldt, Green, and Myerson to ensure that their hyperboloid value function of risk and delay can be used throughout my project. Please try out the stimuli to help me collect pilot data.

Take the gaming user experience satisfaction scale (GUESS)!

Created by Mikki Phan, the GUESS is a satsfaction survey that assesses video game satisfaction on 9 factors:

  • Usability/Playability
  • Narrative
  • Play Engrossment
  • Enjoyment
  • Creative Freedom
  • Audio Aesthetics
  • Visual Aesthetics
  • Personal Gratification
  • Social Connectivity
