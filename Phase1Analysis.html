Results of Phase 1 (WSU Students)

We collected 56 participants using the WSU SONA system to replicate the Vanderveldt findings. Below you will find results for both prospects (i.e., $40k & $800). You should notice the expected hyperboloid trend, and that delay impacts the $800 prospect far more than $40k prospect. Therefore, delaying an outcome has a larger effect on subjective value as you deal with smaller prospects.
